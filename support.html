Support — Grow Your Cat

Grow Your Cat is a calm, single-player focus timer where every focus session grows a one-of-a-kind cat. This page answers the most common questions and tells you how to get in touch.

Contact us

Need help, found a bug, or have an idea? Email growyourcat.app@gmail.com. We read every message and usually reply within a few days. There are no accounts and no support portal — a plain email is all you need.

Frequently asked questions

Where are my cats saved?

Everything — your grown cats, focus sessions, intentions, streaks, and settings — is stored privately on your device. If you are signed in to iCloud, the app also keeps a small automatic backup in your own private iCloud account.

Will I lose my collection if I reinstall or get a new phone?

No, as long as iCloud is enabled for the app. Your collection is restored automatically from your private iCloud backup when you reinstall Grow Your Cat or set it up on a new device signed in to the same Apple ID.

Do my cats sync between my devices?

Yes. If your devices are signed in to the same Apple ID with iCloud enabled, Grow Your Cat keeps your collection in sync across your Apple devices — a cat grown on your iPhone shows up on your iPad automatically. The data stays in your own private iCloud account; the developer cannot see it.

How do I turn iCloud sync off?

Open the iOS Settings app, find Grow Your Cat (or the iCloud section), and disable iCloud for the app. The app keeps working — your data simply stays on the device only.

Do I have to allow notifications?

No. Notifications (for example, when a focus session or break ends) are optional and generated entirely on your device. If you decline the permission, the timer still works fully.

How do I use the Pomodoro features?

By default the app is a simple, free-form timer. In Settings you can opt into each Pomodoro principle individually: break cycles (with an optional longer break every few sessions), focus intentions (tag what you're working on), and mandatory breaks (make breaks unskippable and auto-continue the cycle).

Is the app really free? What is the “Support” link?

Yes — Grow Your Cat is completely free, with no ads and no tracking. The “Support Grow Your Cat” row in Settings opens an optional donation page (Ko-fi) in your browser. It is a tip jar only; it unlocks nothing, and you never have to use it.

Does the app need an internet connection?

No. Grow Your Cat works fully offline. The only background network activity is Apple's iCloud syncing your own data to your own account.

What languages does the app support?

English, Ukrainian, Polish, German, Spanish, Portuguese (Brazil), Japanese, Korean, and Chinese (Simplified). The app follows your device language automatically.
