Privacy Policy — Grow Your Cat

Effective date: 14 June 2026

Grow Your Cat (“the app”) is a single-player focus timer. It is built to be private by design. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

The short version

Grow Your Cat does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Everything the app creates — your grown cats, focus sessions, intentions, streaks, statistics, and settings — is stored in a private database that belongs to the app on your device. The only place this data is also copied is your own private iCloud account, as described next.

iCloud sync & backup

If you are signed in to iCloud on your device, the app automatically stores a small copy of your progress in your own private iCloud account, using Apple’s key-value storage. This happens in the background — for example when you finish a focus session or reopen the app. It serves two purposes: your collection stays in sync across all your Apple devices signed in to the same Apple ID (a cat grown on your iPhone appears on your iPad), and it survives reinstalling the app or moving to a new device. The data lives entirely within your personal iCloud account, governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy; the developer has no access to it and cannot see it. You can turn this off at any time by disabling iCloud for Grow Your Cat in your device’s iCloud settings.

Notifications

The app may ask permission to send you local notifications (for example, when a focus session or break ends). These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any server. You can decline or revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings; the app works fully without it.

No analytics, no tracking, no third parties

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers. It does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA). The app does not send your information to the developer or to any third party. The only background network activity is Apple’s iCloud syncing your own data to your own account, as described under “iCloud backup” above.

External links

The Settings screen includes an optional “Support” link. If — and only if — you tap it, your device opens a third-party donation page (Ko-fi) in your web browser. The whole app is free; this is just a tip jar and unlocks nothing. No personal data is sent when you open it, and any information you choose to provide on that page is handled under Ko-fi’s own privacy policy, not ours.

Children’s privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is rated suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
